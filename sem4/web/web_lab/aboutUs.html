About us

Monkey central website (before 2020 - "Learn monkeys") — a website and a community dedicated to spreading information, research and all things that are related to monkeys. Created in 2004 by a group of enthusiasts, since then this website has been adding to its database of monkeys of all continents and species. Here you can find information about the lifestyle, conservation status, habitat, appearance and other things about each monkey species.

Our activity:

  • Dissemination of information and awareness about monkeys and threats to their life in the realities of the modern world;
  • Writing articles, petitions, appeals and other documents with demands to improve the situation;
  • Organization of thematic events.
